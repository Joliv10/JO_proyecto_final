BENEATH THE FADING TOWN OF DIRTHMOUTH SLEEPS HALLOWNEST, AN ANCIENT, INSECT KINGDOM.

Many are drawn beneath the surface, searching for riches, or glory, or answers to old secrets.

Now something awakens within the ruin, a powerful force that turns bugs mad and robs travellers of their senses.

What is the source of this new plague? What calamity befell this once great kingdom?

To solve these mysteries will require a journey into the deepest depths, but some secrets may be better left buried...

Main Features

EXPLORE A VAST INTERCONECTED WORLD

The world of Hollow Knight is brought to life in vivid, moody detail, its caverns alive with bizarre and terrifying creatures, each animated by hand in a traditional 2D style. Hallownest is expansive and open. Choose which paths you take, which enemies you face and find your own way forward.

ENCOUNTER A BIZARRE COLLECTION OF FRIENDS AND FOES

An enormous cast of cute and creepy characters all brought to life with traditional 2D frame-by-frame animation. Over 140 enemies and 30 epic bosses! Face ferocious beasts and vanquish ancient knights on your quest through the kingdom.Track down every last twisted foe and add them to your Hunter's Journal!

EVOLVE WITH POWERFUL NEW SKILLS AND ABILITIES

Gain spells, strength and speed. Leap to new heights on ethereal wings. Dash forward in a blazing flash. Blast foes with fiery Soul! Equip Charms, ancient relics that offer bizarre new powers and abilities. Choose your favourites and make your journey unique!
